Yu-Chun Ding  

I am currently pursuing my PhD degree at National Tsing Hua University, advised by Prof. Chao-Tsung Huang. My research interests include algorithm exploration, energy-efficient computing architecture design, and digital VLSI system implementation for high-performance embedded vision applications.

I am the first author of a top conference paper and also the co-author of several top conferences and journal papers. I have been involved in several VLSI circuit tape-outs, and have experience in establishing real-time silicon demonstration platforms.
